Install the app

Which device are you using?

Or scan this with your phone camera to open the app

📱 iPhone / iPad

  1. Open the link in Safari (it must be Safari).
  2. Tap the Share button (the square with an up arrow).
  3. Scroll down and tap “Add to Home Screen”.
  4. Tap “Add” — the app icon appears on your home screen.

🤖 Android

  1. Open the link in Chrome.
  2. Tap the “Install app” button if it appears, or the ⋮ menu (top right).
  3. Tap “Install app” / “Add to Home screen”.
  4. Confirm — the app icon appears.

💻 Computer

  1. Open the link in Chrome or Edge.
  2. Click the install icon in the address bar (a small screen with a ⊕), or the menu → “Install The Italian Club”.
  3. Confirm — it opens in its own window and gets a shortcut.
You only do this once per device. After that, just tap the app icon — like any other app.
